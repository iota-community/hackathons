- How to prepare for a Hackathon?
    - What todo on the first day
        - Shape the idea
        - Define tools (use tools you know and like!)
    - What to do on "the middle days"
        - Share knowledge
        - get shit done
        - meet new people
    - What to do on the last day? 
        - Focus on the presentation!
